Our mission

One in three meals never gets eaten.
We think that's worth fixing.

Ules started in Astana with a small idea: restaurants throw away perfectly good food at the end of every day, and people would happily eat it for a fraction of the price. The catch was always logistics — how do you connect them, fast?

So we built an app. Restaurants list what's left. You see what's near you, reserve a bag, and pick it up before they close. Everyone saves something — money, food, a little carbon. It's a small loop, but it adds up.

For people

Find dinner. Save the planet a little.

Open the app, see what nearby restaurants have rescued today, pick a bag, and walk over to collect it. You usually pay between ₸600 and ₸2,000 for food that originally cost two or three times that.

  • Browse rescue bags near you
  • See exactly when to collect
  • Track your impact over time
  • Pay in-app, show the receipt at pickup
For restaurants

Turn surplus into revenue.

Listing takes a minute. List a bag, set a pickup window, and you'll have people walking through your door for food you would have thrown out. We handle payment and customer comms.

  • List bags in seconds
  • Dashboard with daily revenue + reviews
  • Direct payouts in Tenge
  • No upfront cost — we take a small fee per bag
